Generations of Expertise

Sourcing the Finest Gemstones

For generations, VijayKumar Diamonds & Gems has been synonymous with purity, authenticity, and luxury. What began as a passionate pursuit of natural gemstones has evolved into a heritage brand trusted by families and astrologers worldwide.

We specialize in sourcing raw, unheated gemstones directly from conflict-free mines across Ceylon (Sri Lanka), Burma (Myanmar), Zambia, Colombia, and Madagascar. Our in-house certified gemologists inspect every stone for cut, clarity, carat weight, and origin before it is carefully faceted.

Whether you are seeking a loose gemstone for astrological remedies (Navaratna) or purchasing customized fine diamond jewelry, our mission remains unchanged: Real Gems. Real Value.
